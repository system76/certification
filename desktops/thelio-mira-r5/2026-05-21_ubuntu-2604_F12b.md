### Suspend notes and issues

When a single NVMe drive is installed into either M2B_SB or M2C_SB, the system wakes from suspend immedietly.
As long as one NVMe drive is installed in M2A_CPU, the system will suspend and resume properly 
regardless of NVMe presence in other M.2 slots.
